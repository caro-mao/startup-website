Powerlift to conquer Sun & Moon

Our priority is your success. Our team's aim is to provide you with high-quality lever belts, designed to assist you during your lifting sessions.

We are committed to help you go beyond your limits, overcome obstacles, and conquer the sun and moon.

Why "RAHU" ?

Our company's name derives from the Cambodian myth about a strong divine entity: Rahu who is also known as the king of demons.

Rahu tries to swallow the sun or the moon. Rahu is immortal and is believed to be responsible of moon and sun eclipses.

  • Our 10 mm belt is made for you if:

    • You are powerlifting for recreational purposes. It will feel comfortable and will not take as much time to break-in all while supporting your back during your lifts. Fits you better if your torso is shorter.

  • Our 13 mm belt is made for you if:

    • You are an advanced powerlifter, or a heavy bodyweight powerlifter. This will give you all the extra support you need and prevent injuries. Fits you better if your torso is longer.
